The Process

We structure our work into sprints. Think Usain Bolt, but for design. Two one week long sprints crossing the finish line with all of the documents you need to take to your developers.

Developed at Google Ventures, a design sprint is a five-day process for answering critical business questions through design, prototyping, and testing ideas with customers

We adapted this into a two week long process. The first week is focused on discovery & vision. The second week is for creating, producing, and finalizing.
