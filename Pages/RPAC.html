RPAC

RPAC is a quick and useful way for students at the Ohio state university to check the schedule of basketball courts located on campus. Pulling difficult to find data from an OSU recreation center website, the app presents which courts are available for you on any given time.

Overview

As a student at the Ohio State University I had trouble finding the availability of basketball courts without physically going up to the gym and checking the status. This app allowed users to quickly, within 5 seconds, view if there are open courts so that they do not waste time going to the gym. Presented visually, you are able to view which court is referenced by a 3d image with specific courts highlighted.

App Store

Problem

The availability of courts is not widely accessible which leads to wasted time.

Solution

We developed an accessible way for users to view which courts are available within 5 seconds. Providing a 3d visual diagram along with the available time allows the user to easily determine which court is open.
