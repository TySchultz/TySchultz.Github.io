Pick Up

About

Pickup is a fantasy sports application. Think draft kings, fan duel, or fantasy football but the whole season long. The goal is to score as many points as possible by choosing the correct winner of all the NFL games. Join a league and invite your friends to compete and see who knows the most.

Problem

As a casual fan of the NFL it is hard to play a fantasy sports game where it requires knowledge about individual players. There is a barrier to entry for most users.

Solution

Take the competition from individual players to the entire team. This allows much easier engagement as team records and hot streaks provide a clear set of information to a player deciding on the winner.
