Design is about positively impacting lives by helping one user at a time



I'm an interface designer & developer, currently based in Cleveland, Ohio. I work as an independent contractor with a network of friends and agencies across the country. Clients typically hire me for user experience design direction and execution.

Growing up with computers I went to college for computer science. Traditionally trained as an engineer allows me to create realistic and useable interfaces that are easy to build. I enjoy the challenge of blending design & technology. The point when the two meet is where great experiences are made.
