What Is RVS?

The RVS, or Resonant Vibrational Separation, Technology is a breakthrough in rubber materials recycling. Using state-of-the-art processes, the RVS tech allows us cleanly and efficiently convert otherwise useless rejected tire components into a high-quality, low-cost, and environmentally-friendly rubber source for a wide array of applications.

Economically Sound, Environmentally Friendly

  • In the US alone, over 100 million pounds of the specific tire component we work with is being disposed of by means of landfill every year.

  • Besides our RVS Tech, there are currently no existing recycling methods to effectively repurpose the rubber within this material.

  • As a result, RVS Rubber Solutions is in a position to reduce and eventually eliminate an increasingly large source of ecological detriment, while also servicing a rapidly growing market.

Looking Forward

We are always looking to grow. Though we are currently aiming to service a wide market while solving a significant environmental issue, RVS Rubber solutions is constantly thinking ahead. In time, our RVS Tech has the capacity to revolutionize scrap tire recycling. We understand this is a global priority from a pollution standpoint and solving this issue could benefit humanity for generations to come. Furthermore, this could prove to be a massive usable rubber source, allowing us to service the rubber industry to a greater magnitude. As a result, we are working diligently to expand our technology into this field to solve such a prominent problem and continue to advance our business.
