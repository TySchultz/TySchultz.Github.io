1. Kamala Harris Solidifies Campaign

Kamala Harris receives broad Democratic support and significant funds following Biden's exit from the presidential race.

Summary:

On July 22, 2024, San Francisco Mayor London Breed spoke at a rally supporting Vice President Kamala Harris at City Hall. The rally came on the same day President Biden announced his withdrawal from the 2024 presidential race, simultaneously endorsing Harris as the Democratic nominee. This pivotal moment was highlighted by the unanimous vote of the California delegation of the Democratic National Committee, which backed Harris for the presidency.


"Kamala Harris represents a new generation of leadership and has the ability to unite our party and beat Trump in November,"

- Rusty Hicks, state Democratic Party Chair


Immediately following Biden’s endorsement, Harris raised a record-breaking $81 million in just 24 hours, reflecting her strong financial and political support. This significant fundraising achievement indicates a robust backing from supporters and places Harris in a commanding position for the upcoming presidential race. The overwhelming financial contributions underscore the confidence that her supporters and the Democratic Party have in her candidacy.

The rally at City Hall, featuring Mayor London Breed, symbolized a critical unification of political forces in California, demonstrating widespread solidarity within the Democratic Party. This moment not only underscored Harris’s rising prominence within the party but also illustrated the strategic alignment aimed at bolstering her campaign. The coordinated endorsements and financial backing are expected to play a crucial role in shaping the dynamics of the 2024 presidential election.
