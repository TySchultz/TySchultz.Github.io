1. Trump Names Vance Running Mate

Donald Trump announces Ohio Senator J.D. Vance as his vice presidential pick amidst a surge in cryptocurrency following an assassination attempt on Trump.

Summary:

Former President Donald Trump has named Ohio Senator J.D. Vance as his running mate for the upcoming presidential election. This decision has garnered praise from several Republican lawmakers due to Vance's stance on competition and antitrust laws. Vance, who initially was a critic of Trump, evolved into a strong supporter and crucial ally, now forming a significant part of Trump's campaign. This announcement was made during the Republican National Convention (RNC), marking Trump's first public appearance since surviving an assassination attempt in Butler, Pennsylvania, over the weekend.


I have decided that the person best suited to assume the position of Vice President of the United States is Senator J.D. Vance of the Great State of Ohio.

- Donald Trump


The assassination attempt had broader implications, particularly in the financial markets, with a notable surge in Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies. Bitcoin soared over 10%, reaching $63,000, as traders speculated the increased probability of a Republican victory under Trump's leadership. Grayscale's GBTC reported an inflow increase totaling $23 million, highlighting the intensified interest in cryptocurrency investments amidst political unrest and prospects.

Trump's selection of Vance has been welcomed within Republican circles and has added enthusiasm within the cryptocurrency community due to Trump and Vance's perceived support for digital currencies. Financial experts and political analysts alike are closely watching how this decision will influence both the impending political outcomes and the further fluctuations in cryptocurrency markets.
