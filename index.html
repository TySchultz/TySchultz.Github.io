RPAC

RPAC is a quick and useful way for students at the Ohio state university to check the schedule of basketball courts located on campus. Pulling difficult to find data from an OSU recreation center website, the app presents which courts are available for you to play on at a given time.

PickUp

Pickup is a fantasy sports application. Think draft kings, fan duel, or fantasy football but the whole season long. The goal is to score as many points as possible by choosing the correct winner of all the NFL games. Join a league and invite your friends to compete and see who knows the most.
