1. Haley Endorses Vance

Nikki Haley formally endorses J.D. Vance as Donald Trump’s vice-presidential pick.

Summary:

Nikki Haley has announced her endorsement of J.D. Vance as the vice-presidential candidate alongside Donald Trump. This marks a significant shift from her prior criticisms of Vance. In her endorsement speech, she conveyed strong support for Trump and indicated Vance would be a vital asset in their political campaign against Joe Biden and Kamala Harris.


Donald Trump has my strong endorsement, period.

- Nikki Haley


Haley’s statements emphasized the importance of strong leadership and foreign policy, hinting at the potential implications if the Democratic party secures a victory in the upcoming election. Vance echoed Haley’s sentiments, expressing that agreeing completely with Trump is not a requisite for supporting his overall agenda. Notably, Vance has been critical of a $60 billion military aid package for Ukraine, aligning with a more cautious foreign policy stance.

The funds, proposed during his time in office, were blocked, revealing his dissent in such fiscal decisions. Additionally, Vance’s memoir, 'Hillbilly Elegy,' remains popular, topping Amazon’s bestseller lists and seeing a film adaptation release in 2020.
